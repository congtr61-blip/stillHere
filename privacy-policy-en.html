FAQ

Q: Will you sell my data?

A: No. We never sell any personal data to anyone.

Q: Do you track my location?

A: No. We collect IP addresses for security but never track physical location.

Q: What happens when I delete my account?

A: All personal data is deleted immediately. Only legally required data is retained per applicable laws.

Q: What encryption do you use?

A: Transit uses TLS/SSL (HTTPS), storage uses AES-256 encryption. This is the highest industry standard.

Q: How do I update my information?

A: Select "Account Settings" in the app and modify your information.
